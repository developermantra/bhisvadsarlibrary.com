BriBooks

BriBooks is the world’s leading children creative writing platform, enabling children of all ages to learn creative writing, publish their books online, and sell printed-on-demand books on BriBooks.com and Amazon.com in one click. To our pleasure, Billabong High International School, Vadsar has been registered with BriBooks. This organization conducts book fairs, book festivals, competitions and a lot of other exciting events. It also gives students an opportunity to publish their books and earn money that too for free. Our student published over 84 books.
